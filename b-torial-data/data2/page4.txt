<h1>Step 4: Members</h1>

<p>The <span class="code">!</span> symbol is used for <span class="term">unordered members</span>.</p>

<p>The <span class="code">@</span> symbol is used for <span class="term">ordered members</span>.</p>

<p>In this diagram, the members are ordered and
identified by sequential numbers.</p>

<p>The addresses of the three entity members in the graph are:</p>

<p class="indent">
<span class="code">=alice[#event]@0
=alice[#event]@1
=alice[#event]@2
</span>
