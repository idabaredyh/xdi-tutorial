<h1>Step 1: Peer roots and inner roots</h1>

<p>Tutorials 1 and 2 covered the basics of the XDI Graph Model, including
the types of node and arcs, and some of the functions of context nodes, such
as entities, attributes, values, collections, and members.</p>

<p>In the beginning, we saw that every graph starts with a common root node.
In this Tutorial 3, we will explain two other types of root nodes: Peer roots
and inner roots.</p>
