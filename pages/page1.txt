<h1>Step 1: Graph Model</h1>

<p>XDI is a technology for modeling, storing, and manipulating data.</p>
<p>It fits into a similar category of technologies as JSON, XML and RDF, but also has a number
of properties that distinguishes it.</p>
<p>XDI is a <span class="term">graph-based data model</span>. This means that all data is expressed using nodes and
arcs in a graph. At a minimum, a graph consists of a single node, called the 
<span class="term">common root node</span>.</p>
