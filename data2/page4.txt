<h1>Step 4: Instances</h1>

<p>The <span class="xri">!</span> symbol is used for unordered instances.</p>

<p>The <span class="xri">#</span> symbol is used for ordered instances.</p>

<p>In this diagram, the instances are ordered and
identified by sequential numbers.</p>

<p>The addresses of the three entity instances in the graph are:</p>

<p class="indent">
<span class="xri">=alice[+event]#0
=alice[+event]#1
=alice[+event]#2
</span>
